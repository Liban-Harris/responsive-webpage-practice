No Sugar. No Oil. All Real Food.

We are northern ohio's first oil-free and sugar-free granola company.

We believe in having not only good tasting food, but nutritious food that makes you feel good.

Our granola is made with the best ingredients we can find and made with love in our own home in Shaker Heights.

Being Sugar free, we use whole dates as our sweetener of choice. None of that prossessed sugar in any of its forms here. Please take a look at our available products to find out more about our amazing and unique flavours.
